## Grading

One can obtain 40 points in total for:
- 2 large test (15 points each);
- 6 small tests (1 point each), roughly every 2 weeks;
- Activity (4 points in total).

The sum of points will be transformed to the final grade according to the scheme:
- 21-24 -> 3.0
- 25-28 -> 3.5
- 29-32 -> 4.0
- 33-36 -> 4.5
- 37-40 -> 5.0
